A list of the events a module can send to the other modules through the event bus.

ImageRendered (imagedata)
    Sent when the renderer has finished rendering an image.
Who sends it:
    Renderer
Who subscribes to it:
    WindowManager

SceneUpdated (scenedata)
    Sent when the scene has been updated.
Who sends it:
    SceneManager
Who subscribes to it:
    Renderer

ExitApplication ()
    Sent when the application is requested to stop.
Who sends it:
    Any module or the application itself
Who subscribes to it:
    The main application
